📢 Message from the Director

Empowering Every Student
to Reach Their Potential

"Our Placement Management System is built on a simple belief — every student deserves a platform that works as hard as they do. We have invested in technology and industry partnerships so that talent finds its rightful place in the professional world."

The Training & Placement Cell serves as the bridge between our students and the industry. We work tirelessly to facilitate interactions with top-tier companies, conduct skill-development workshops, mock interviews, and aptitude training to ensure every eligible student walks away with a rewarding offer.

I encourage all students to actively participate in placement drives, keep their profiles updated, and leverage this platform to its fullest. Your success is our greatest achievement.

— Prof. Dr. Rajendra Kumar, Director T&P
